Needs vs Wants

Understanding the difference between needs and wants is a fundamental pillar of financial literacy. Needs are essential for survival such as food, water, and shelter. Wants are things that improve comfort or enjoyment but are not necessary for survival.

Learning this helps you prioritize spending, avoid waste, and make smarter financial decisions for your future.
