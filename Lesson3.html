Lesson 3: Budgeting & Saving

Budgeting & Saving

Budgeting helps you manage your money by planning income and expenses. Saving means keeping money aside for future goals or emergencies.

1. You earn $200 per week and save 25%. How much do you save?

2. You spend $50 on food and $30 on transport. What is your total spending?

3. If you save $100 per month, how much do you save in 6 months?

4. You have $500. After spending $175, how much is left?
