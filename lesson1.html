Lesson 1: What is Financial Literacy?

What is Financial Literacy?

Financial literacy means understanding how money works. It includes knowing how to earn, save, spend, and invest money wisely. Being financially literate helps you make smart decisions and avoid common money mistakes.

It is an essential life skill that helps you manage your future, plan for goals, and stay out of debt.
